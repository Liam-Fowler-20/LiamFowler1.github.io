The NBA's
Least Winning Archetype

I have been a basketball fan for awhile and my least favorite archetype of player is the so-called bucket getter that scores at a very low efficiency and also is a below average defender. Many players have this archetype, they usually can make very tough shots that look cool; however, they also take too many of these shots and don't get easy points. I personally believe that these are not winning basketball players, so I would like to see if the data backs this up.
